No. You keep your existing business number and set up a simple call forward. Enterprise plans support direct Twilio passthrough so there's zero change in the caller experience.

Frontline identifies urgent situations and handles them by your rules — direct text alert, priority email flag, or a custom emergency script you define during your 15-minute intake. Nothing falls through.
